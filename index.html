Frequently Asked Questions

What are your operating hours?
We are open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
What is your staff-to-child ratio?
We maintain state-recommended ratios: 1:4 for infants, 1:6 for toddlers, and 1:10 for preschoolers.
Do you provide meals?
Yes, we provide nutritious breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks.
What is your sick child policy?
Children must be fever-free for 24 hours without medication before returning to daycare.
